FAQs

How do I create a new wallet?

To create a new wallet, navigate to the "My Wallets" page and click the "Add New Wallet" button. Fill in the required card details including card number, cardholder name, expiry date, and CVV. Note that the number of wallets you can create depends on your account verification level. Tier 1 users can have one wallet, while fully verified users can create multiple wallets.

What are the daily transaction limits?

Transaction limits vary by account tier:
- Tier 1 (Basic): $50 per transaction, 1 wallet maximum
- Tier 2 (Email Verified): Unlimited transfers, 1 wallet maximum
- Tier 3 (Fully Verified): Unlimited transfers, unlimited wallets

To increase your limits, complete the verification process in your profile settings.

How do I verify my account?

To verify your account, go to your Profile page and select "Verification". You'll need to verify your email first by clicking the link sent to your registered email address. For full verification (Tier 3), you'll need to upload a photo of your ID document (passport or driver's license) and a selfie. Verification is usually processed within 24-48 hours.

How do I send money to another user?

To send money to another user, go to the "Transfer" page and select "P2P Transfer". You can send money either by entering the recipient's username or by scanning their QR code. Enter the amount you wish to send and add an optional note. You can also schedule the transfer for a future date if needed. The transfer will be processed immediately unless scheduled.

Are there any fees for using SI-Wallet?

SI-Wallet charges minimal fees:
- Deposits: Free
- P2P Transfers: Free
- Withdrawals: 1% of the amount (minimum $0.50)
- Currency conversion: 2.5% of the transaction amount

All fees are displayed before you confirm any transaction.

How secure is SI-Wallet?

SI-Wallet employs bank-level security measures to protect your data and funds. We use end-to-end encryption for all transactions, two-factor authentication for account access, and regular security audits. Your card details are encrypted and never stored in plain text. We also monitor accounts for suspicious activity and provide instant notifications for all transactions.
